I am a Research Assistant at North South University working at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, and Multimodal AI. My research interests include low-resource language technologies, multilingual AI, LLM alignment and evaluation, representation learning, and benchmark construction. I am particularly interested in developing robust and accessible AI systems for underrepresented languages and plan to pursue a PhD focused on advancing language and multimodal intelligence.
